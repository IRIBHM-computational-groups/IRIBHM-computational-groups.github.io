Research

Currently, I am dedicated to my master thesis project related to the the histological quantitative trait loci. I am exploring the genomic relationships with histology, employing an in-lab developped AI pipeline.My upcoming PhD project, titled "Single-cell Resolution 3D Molecular Histology of Papillary Thyroid Cancer and Adjacent Healthy Tissues" aims to construct a 3D model of papillary thyroid cancer (PTC) and healthy thyroid tissues. The project bridges the gap between 3D histology and molecular biology techniques, incorporating spatial transcriptomics, snRNA-seq, and spatially resolved genomics. If you want to know more about my research, do not hesitate to come and talk to me !
